Surabhi is operated by SDSURABHI INFRA PRIVATE LIMITED (CIN: U41002UP2023PTC194590), a registered Indian company incorporated on 26th December 2023. Our mission is to democratize cricket strategy education and make fantasy sports accessible to every cricket enthusiast, regardless of their financial background.
