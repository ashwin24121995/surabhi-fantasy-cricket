Our Commitment to Fair Play

At Surabhi, we believe that fair play is the foundation of an enjoyable fantasy cricket experience. Our Fair Play Policy outlines the principles, rules, and measures we implement to ensure that every user has an equal opportunity to compete, learn, and enjoy the game.

Our Promise: We are committed to maintaining a platform where success is determined by skill, knowledge, and strategic thinking‚Äînot by unfair advantages or rule violations.

Skill-Based Competition

Fantasy cricket on Surabhi is a game of skill. Success depends on your cricket knowledge, analytical abilities, and strategic decision-making.

Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited on our platform. Engaging in any of these activities will result in disciplinary action.

1. Multiple Accounts

Each user is permitted to maintain only ONE (1) account on the Platform.

  • Creating multiple accounts to gain unfair advantages
  • Using accounts registered by others
  • Sharing account credentials with others
  • Operating accounts on behalf of others

High Severity - Results in permanent ban of all associated accounts.

2. Collusion

Coordinating with other users to gain unfair advantages is prohibited.

  • Sharing team selections before contest deadlines
  • Coordinating team compositions to maximize collective advantage
  • Creating teams designed to benefit another user's team
  • Any form of organized coordination between users

High Severity - Results in permanent ban of all involved accounts.

3. Automation & Bots

Using automated tools or software to interact with the platform is prohibited.

  • Using bots to create teams or join contests
  • Automated scripts for data scraping
  • Third-party tools that automate platform interactions
  • Browser extensions that manipulate platform functionality

High Severity - Results in permanent ban.

4. Exploitation of Bugs or Vulnerabilities

Exploiting technical issues for personal gain is prohibited.

  • Exploiting bugs to gain unfair advantages
  • Manipulating scoring calculations
  • Exploiting timing vulnerabilities
  • Any attempt to manipulate contest outcomes

High Severity - Results in permanent ban and potential legal action.

Note: If you discover a bug or vulnerability, please report it to us at support@skillarenasports.com. Responsible disclosure is appreciated and may be rewarded.

5. Insider Information

Using non-public information to gain advantages is prohibited.

  • Using insider information about team selections before public announcement
  • Using information obtained through connections with players, teams, or officials
  • Any use of material non-public information

High Severity - Results in permanent ban.

6. Identity Fraud

Misrepresenting your identity is prohibited.

  • Providing false personal information during registration
  • Using another person's identity documents
  • Impersonating other users or public figures
  • Misrepresenting your age or location

High Severity - Results in permanent ban.

7. Harassment & Abuse

Treating other users with disrespect is prohibited.

  • Harassing, threatening, or bullying other users
  • Using offensive, discriminatory, or hateful language
  • Posting inappropriate content
  • Spamming or sending unsolicited messages

Medium Severity - Results in warning, suspension, or ban depending on severity.

8. Manipulation of Leaderboards

Any attempt to artificially inflate rankings is prohibited.

  • Creating fake accounts to boost rankings
  • Coordinating with others to manipulate standings
  • Any artificial manipulation of leaderboard positions

High Severity - Results in permanent ban.

Detection & Enforcement

How We Detect Violations

We employ multiple methods to detect fair play violations:

  • Automated Systems: Advanced algorithms that monitor for suspicious patterns and activities
  • Manual Review: Our team reviews flagged accounts and activities
  • User Reports: We investigate reports from other users
  • Pattern Analysis: Analysis of team compositions, timing, and behavior patterns
  • Device Fingerprinting: Detection of multiple accounts from same devices

Consequences of Violations

Depending on the nature and severity of the violation, consequences may include:

Severity First Offense Repeat Offense
Low Warning Temporary suspension (7 days)
Medium Temporary suspension (7-30 days) Permanent ban
High Permanent ban Permanent ban + Legal action

Important: All decisions regarding fair play violations are made at our sole discretion. We reserve the right to take any action we deem necessary to maintain the integrity of our platform.

Appeals Process

If you believe a decision regarding your account was made in error, you may appeal:

  1. Submit an Appeal: Email support@skillarenasports.com with subject line "Fair Play Appeal - [Your Username]"
  2. Provide Details: Include your account details and a clear explanation of why you believe the decision was incorrect
  3. Review Period: Our team will review your appeal within 14 business days
  4. Final Decision: You will be notified of the outcome via email

Please note that our decision on appeals is final and binding.

Reporting Violations

We encourage all users to report suspected fair play violations. Your reports help us maintain a fair environment for everyone.

How to Report

  • Email: support@skillarenasports.com with subject "Fair Play Report"
  • Include the username(s) of the suspected violator(s)
  • Describe the suspected violation in detail
  • Provide any evidence or screenshots if available

Confidentiality

All reports are treated confidentially. We will not disclose the identity of reporters to the reported users.

Fantasy Cricket: A Game of Skill

Fantasy cricket as offered on Surabhi is recognized as a game of skill. Success on our platform depends on:

  • Cricket Knowledge: Understanding of the game, players, and strategies
  • Statistical Analysis: Ability to analyze player statistics and performance data
  • Strategic Thinking: Making informed decisions about team composition
  • Research: Staying updated on team news, pitch conditions, and weather
  • Risk Assessment: Balancing safe picks with differential selections

The outcome is not determined by chance but by the skill and judgment of the participant.

Updates to This Policy

  • For significant changes, we will notify users via email or platform notification
  • Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
