Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Surabhi fantasy cricket platform.

üéÆ General Questions

What is Surabhi?
Surabhi is a FREE-TO-PLAY fantasy cricket platform operated by SDSURABHI INFRA PRIVATE LIMITED. It allows cricket enthusiasts to create virtual teams of real players and compete on leaderboards based on actual match performances. Our platform is designed purely for entertainment and educational purposes with no real money involved.
Is Surabhi really free to play?
Yes, absolutely! Surabhi is 100% free to play. There are no entry fees, no deposits, no hidden charges, and no real money prizes. You compete purely for leaderboard rankings and the satisfaction of testing your cricket knowledge against other enthusiasts.
Who can play on Surabhi?
Anyone who is 18 years or older and resides in India can play on Surabhi. However, users from Andhra Pradesh, Assam, Nagaland, Odisha, Sikkim, and Telangana are not permitted to participate due to government compliance requirements in these states.
Is fantasy cricket legal in India?
Fantasy sports have been recognized as games of skill by various Indian courts. Since Surabhi is a free-to-play platform with no real money involved, it operates as an educational and entertainment platform. We maintain strict compliance with all applicable laws and regulations.

üìù Registration & Account

How do I create an account?
Creating an account is simple and free. Click the "Register" button, enter your full name, email address, create a password, confirm your age (18+), and select your state of residence. Verify your email, and you're ready to play!
Why do I need to verify my age?
Age verification is mandatory as our platform is only available to users who are 18 years or older. This is part of our commitment to responsible gaming practices and compliance with Indian regulations.
I forgot my password. What should I do?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.
Can I have multiple accounts?
No, each user is allowed only one account. Creating multiple accounts violates our Fair Play Policy and may result in permanent suspension of all associated accounts.

üèè Gameplay

How do I create a fantasy team?
Select a match from the upcoming matches list, then choose 11 players from both teams within your 100-credit budget. Your team must include 1-4 wicketkeepers, 3-6 batsmen, 1-4 all-rounders, and 3-6 bowlers. Finally, select a Captain (2x points) and Vice-Captain (1.5x points).
What is the team composition rule?
Your team of 11 players must include: 1-4 Wicketkeepers, 3-6 Batsmen, 1-4 All-rounders, and 3-6 Bowlers. Additionally, you can select a maximum of 7 players from any single team.
Can I edit my team after creating it?
Yes, you can edit your team any number of times before the match deadline. Once the match starts, no further changes are allowed.
What happens if a player in my team doesn't play?
If a player in your team doesn't participate in the match, they will earn 0 points. That's why it's important to check team announcements and playing XI before the deadline.

üìä Scoring & Leaderboard

How are points calculated?
Points are calculated based on actual player performances in the match. Batting points include runs, boundaries, and milestones. Bowling points include wickets, maidens, and economy bonuses. Fielding points include catches, stumpings, and run-outs. Visit our "How to Play" page for the complete scoring system.
What does the Captain and Vice-Captain do?
Your Captain earns 2x (double) points based on their performance, while your Vice-Captain earns 1.5x points. Choosing the right Captain and Vice-Captain is crucial for maximizing your team's score.
How does the leaderboard work?
The leaderboard ranks all participants based on their total team points. Rankings are updated in real-time as the match progresses. The higher your team scores, the better your leaderboard position.
Are there any prizes for winning?
Surabhi is a free-to-play platform with no real money prizes. You compete for leaderboard rankings, recognition, and the satisfaction of testing your cricket knowledge. It's about glory and bragging rights, not money!
