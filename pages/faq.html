Gameplay & Teams

How do I create a fantasy team?

To create a fantasy team:

  • Select an upcoming match from the Matches section
  • Click "Create Team" to start building your squad
  • Select 11 players from both teams following the composition rules
  • Choose your Captain (2x points) and Vice-Captain (1.5x points)
  • Save your team and join a contest

For detailed instructions, visit our How to Play page.

What are the team composition rules?

Your fantasy team must follow these composition rules:

  • Total Players: Exactly 11 players
  • Wicket-keepers (WK): Minimum 1, Maximum 4
  • Batsmen (BAT): Minimum 3, Maximum 6
  • All-rounders (AR): Minimum 1, Maximum 4
  • Bowlers (BOWL): Minimum 3, Maximum 6
  • Per Team Limit: Maximum 7 players from one team

Can I edit my team after creating it?

Yes, you can edit your team unlimited times before the contest deadline. The deadline is typically when the match starts (first ball is bowled).

We recommend:

  • Waiting for the toss result before finalizing your team
  • Checking the official playing XI announcements
  • Making last-minute changes based on pitch conditions and weather

Important: Once the deadline passes, your team is locked and cannot be modified.

What happens if a player in my team doesn't play?

If a player you selected doesn't participate in the actual match (not in the playing XI), they will score 0 points for your team. There are no automatic substitutions.

To avoid this situation:

  • Wait for official playing XI announcements before the deadline
  • Follow team news and injury updates
  • Edit your team to replace players who are not playing

Scoring & Points

How are fantasy points calculated?

Fantasy points are calculated based on players' actual performance in the real match. Points are awarded for:

  • Batting: Runs, boundaries (4s and 6s), half-centuries, centuries
  • Bowling: Wickets, maiden overs, 3/4/5 wicket hauls, economy rate bonuses
  • Fielding: Catches, stumpings, run-outs

Point values vary by match format (T20, ODI, Test). Visit our How to Play page for the complete scoring table.

What is the Captain and Vice-Captain bonus?

The Captain and Vice-Captain selections are crucial for maximizing your team's score:

  • Captain (C): Earns 2x points for all their actions
  • Vice-Captain (VC): Earns 1.5x points for all their actions

For example, if your Captain scores 50 points based on their performance, they'll contribute 100 points to your team total. Choose players you expect to perform exceptionally well.

When are points updated?

Points are updated in near real-time during the match. You can track your team's performance and leaderboard position as the match progresses.

Note: Final points are calculated after the match ends and official statistics are confirmed. There may be minor adjustments if any scoring corrections are made by official sources.
