Master the art of fantasy cricket with our comprehensive guide. Build winning teams, understand scoring, and climb the leaderboards.

What is Fantasy Cricket?

Fantasy cricket is a strategic game where you become the team manager. You select real cricket players to form your virtual team, and based on their actual performance in real matches, your team earns points. The better your players perform on the field, the higher your team scores on the leaderboard.

On Surabhi, fantasy cricket is completely free to play. There are no entry fees, no deposits, and no real money involved. You compete purely for leaderboard rankings, bragging rights, and the satisfaction of outsmarting fellow cricket enthusiasts with your strategic skills.

Whether you're a cricket expert or just starting your journey, Surabhi provides an educational platform to test your cricket knowledge, learn player analytics, and develop strategic thinking‚Äîall without any financial risk.

Team Selection Strategies

Improve your fantasy cricket skills with these expert strategies.

Research Player Form

Always check recent performance statistics before selecting players. A player in good form is more likely to score well. Look at their last 5-10 matches, especially in similar conditions.

Analyze Pitch Conditions

Different pitches favor different players. Spin-friendly pitches benefit spinners and batsmen who play spin well. Fast, bouncy pitches favor pace bowlers and aggressive batsmen.

Head-to-Head Records

Some players perform exceptionally well against specific opponents. Check head-to-head statistics to identify players who historically dominate against the opposition team.

Venue Statistics

Players often perform differently at different venues. Some batsmen have favorite grounds where they consistently score big, while some bowlers excel at specific venues.

Toss Impact

The toss can significantly impact player selection. If a team bats first, their batsmen might score more. If they bowl first, their bowlers might get more wickets on a fresh pitch.

All-Rounder Value

All-rounders can earn points from both batting and bowling, making them valuable picks. Consider making consistent all-rounders your Captain or Vice-Captain for maximum points.

Contest Rules & Guidelines

Ensure fair play by understanding these important rules.

Team Editing

Before Deadline

You can edit your team unlimited times before the contest deadline. Make changes based on toss results, playing XI announcements, and last-minute news.

After Deadline

Once the contest deadline passes (usually at match start), your team is locked. No further changes can be made.

Player Substitutions

If a player in your team doesn't play in the actual match, they will score 0 points. There are no automatic substitutions.
