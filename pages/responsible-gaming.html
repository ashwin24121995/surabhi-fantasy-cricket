Our Commitment to Responsible Gaming

At Surabhi, we believe that fantasy cricket should be a fun, educational, and entertaining experience. We are committed to promoting responsible gaming practices and ensuring that our platform remains a safe and enjoyable environment for all users.

Remember: Surabhi is a 100% FREE-TO-PLAY platform. There is no real money involved, no deposits, and no cash prizes. Our platform is designed purely for entertainment and educational purposes.

Healthy Gaming Habits

Even though our platform is free and involves no financial risk, we encourage all users to maintain healthy gaming habits:

Time Management

  • Set limits on how much time you spend on the platform
  • Take regular breaks during extended sessions
  • Don't let gaming interfere with work, studies, or relationships
  • Maintain a balanced lifestyle with other activities and hobbies
  • Avoid playing when you should be sleeping

Emotional Well-being

  • Remember that fantasy cricket is meant to be fun
  • Don't let contest outcomes affect your mood significantly
  • Avoid chasing leaderboard positions obsessively
  • Take a break if you feel frustrated or stressed
  • Celebrate learning and improvement, not just rankings

Social Balance

  • Maintain healthy relationships outside of gaming
  • Don't neglect family, friends, or social activities
  • Use the platform to connect with fellow cricket enthusiasts positively
  • Avoid excessive competition that damages relationships

Recognizing Problematic Gaming

While our platform is free and doesn't involve gambling, it's important to recognize signs that gaming might be affecting your life negatively:

Warning Signs

  • Spending excessive time on the platform at the expense of other responsibilities
  • Feeling anxious or irritable when unable to access the platform
  • Neglecting work, studies, or personal relationships
  • Losing interest in other hobbies and activities
  • Experiencing sleep disturbances due to gaming
  • Feeling the need to constantly check rankings or results
  • Lying to others about how much time you spend gaming

If you recognize these signs: Consider taking a break from the platform. Talk to someone you trust about your concerns. If needed, seek professional help.

Tools & Features for Responsible Gaming

We provide several tools to help you maintain healthy gaming habits:

Account Controls

  • Self-Exclusion: You can request temporary or permanent account deactivation at any time
  • Activity History: View your participation history to monitor your activity levels
  • Notification Settings: Control the frequency and type of notifications you receive

Support Options

  • Customer Support: Our team is available to help with any concerns
  • Account Deletion: Request complete deletion of your account and data
  • Cool-off Period: Request a temporary break from the platform

Self-Exclusion Options

If you feel you need a break from the platform, we offer the following options:

Temporary Break (Cool-off Period)

Request a temporary deactivation of your account for a specified period (7 days, 30 days, 90 days). During this period:

  • You will not be able to log in to your account
  • You will not receive any notifications or emails
  • Your account and data will be preserved
  • You can request reactivation after the cool-off period ends

Permanent Self-Exclusion

Request permanent deactivation of your account. This action:

  • Is irreversible
  • Results in permanent account closure
  • Prevents creation of new accounts

To request self-exclusion, contact us at support@skillarenasports.com with the subject line "Self-Exclusion Request."

Information for Parents & Guardians

If you are a parent or guardian concerned about a young person's gaming habits:

Prevention

  • Ensure your child is at least 18 years old before allowing them to use fantasy sports platforms
  • Monitor your child's online activities
  • Set clear rules about screen time and gaming
  • Encourage a balance of activities including physical exercise and social interaction

Communication

  • Talk openly with your child about responsible gaming
  • Discuss the difference between free-to-play and real-money platforms
  • Be aware of the signs of problematic gaming
  • Create a supportive environment where your child can discuss concerns

If You Suspect Underage Use

If you believe a minor is using our platform, please contact us immediately at support@skillarenasports.com. We take underage access seriously and will investigate all reports.
